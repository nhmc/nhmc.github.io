This page has a calculator useful for answering those questions that come up when you're in talks or writing proposals, like 'How many kpc correspond to 1 arcminute at redshift 3?', 'How bright is an L* galaxy at redshift 1.5?' or 'What is the lookback time to redshift 2?'.

For a nice introduction to calculating useful cosmological quantities see Hogg (1999) and the references therein. The default parameters below are taken from the WMAP7 results (ADS). A flat cosmology is assumed.
